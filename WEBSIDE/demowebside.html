Our Story

Founded in 2015

The Rusty Spoon began with a simple mission: to serve honest food in a warm environment. We believe that good food brings people together.


Our chefs work directly with local farmers to ensure that every plate is fresh, sustainable, and bursting with flavor. Whether you are here for a quick coffee or a long dinner, we promise an unforgettable experience.
