Project Overview

In this project, I worked on a sales dataset using Microsoft Excel. The main goal was to understand sales performance, find important customers, and create a dashboard to show results clearly.

Tasks Performed

1.Used Conditional Formatting to highlight the Top 10 customers based on total purchase amount.

2.Used What-If Analysis to check how changing the discount affects total profit.

3.Used Data Analysis Toolpak to run Linear Regression (Profit vs Sales) to see the relation between sales and profit.

4.Used Descriptive Statistics to calculate average, minimum, maximum, and other basic values of the dataset.

5.Calculated monthly sales growth and added up/down arrows to show increase or decrease in sales.

6.Created a timestamp column using the NOW() function.

7.Used INDEX(), MATCH(), and filters to find high-value customers.

8.Created Pivot Tables to analyze total sales by region and product.

9.Created bar, line, and pie charts to show important KPIs.

10.Created a dashboard to show all important results in one place.
